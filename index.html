Digimon:
Digital Adventures

Digimon: Digital Adventures, or DDA for short, is a non-profit TTRPG made by fans, for fans, out of our love for Digimon. It’s a long-standing project that many people have had a hand in to have their own Digimon Adventures alongside their friends, both real and digital.

With DDA, you play two characters: the Human, and the Digimon partner! Human Characters can issue orders and help their Digimon in combat, while Digimon can be built to do almost anything!

How Does DDA Work?

The DDA System works with d6s (six-sided dice) used in either skill checks or larger pool checks. Aside from that, the system is largely narrative-based, and is most enjoyable when both the players and GM are collaborating with one another!

The brunt of the mechanics of the system take place when building for combat, where you can take the time to customize your Digimon to be capable of doing anything that you’ve seen within the show itself!
